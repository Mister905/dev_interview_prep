Elevator pitch

  • Full-stack developer, 6+ years; React, TypeScript, Node, SQL
  • Owned features end to end; designing databases, backend APIs, front end UIs, and managing deployments
  • Collaborated with product managers, QA analysts, and external partners and stakeholders

Why this role?

  • Develop modern full-stack applications while leveraging my experience to simplify onboarding and make meaningful contributions from the start

Next role?

  • I want to take ownership of work that matters, keep growing my skills, and be part of a team that values clear communication, accountability, and honest feedback

Why did you choose software development?

  • I enjoy turning complex problems into real, tangible solutions, and software gives me more chances to do that than history or political science. That said, my analytical and research background really helps me break down requirements and create clear, maintainable technical documentation

Why did you leave your last role?

  • I was ready for a new challenge, but the role was affected by budget changes. Since then, I’ve been doing freelance consulting while looking for the right full-time opportunity

What consulting work have you done?

  • Built a dashboard for a short-term rental business to track competitors
  • Used React, TypeScript, NestJS, Supabase
  • Features: manage listings, on-demand scraping, side-by-side snapshot comparison
  • Purpose: help the client understand competitor trends and improve marketing decisions

How do you use AI in your workflow?

  • AI to speed up work without replacing my experience and judgment
  • Sequential workflow: codify business logic → break project into features → plan → implement → code review in isolated agent context (focused feedback) → fix issues
  • Keeps code focused, bugs isolated, reviews clear, and progress easy to track

Where do you see yourself?

  • Take on increasingly complex projects
  • Contribute to key architectural decisions
  • Mentor junior developers
  • Value mentorship because it shaped my own growth

Complex Full Stack Project Leadership

  • Civix: Built claims document submission and approval for NJ Office of Emergency Management within FEMA grant management software
  • Challenge: Enforced strict validation across multi-step approval workflows
  • Stack: React front end, NestJS API, SQL schema to ensure compliance
  • Collaboration: Partnered with QA and stakeholders to create an intuitive UX despite rigid regulatory constraints

Teamwork

  • Enjoy collaborating on code reviews, pair programming, and architecture discussions
  • Give constructive feedback generously and receive it graciously; resolve disagreements by focusing on solutions, not personalities

Prioritization

  • Evaluate competing priorities by their business value and time-sensitivity
  • Focus on blocking tasks first to keep teammates moving forward

Ambiguity

  • Clarify goals and constraints, break work into smaller parts, document assumptions, and align proactively with stakeholders

Communicating with stakeholders/clients

  • Focus on outcomes and trade-offs, not implementation
  • Transparent about risks and willing to provide diplomatic pushback to flawed assumptions
  • Using simple language on video calls, screen recordings or sketches for visual references

Questions to ask the Interviewer

  • What are the team’s most important projects or priorities right now?
  • What are the next steps in the interview process?

Closing line

  • Thanks for your time today. I’d be happy to continue the process and talk more.
